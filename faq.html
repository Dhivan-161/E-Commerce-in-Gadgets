General Questions

All of our main products (smartphones, laptops, smartwatches) come with a standard 1-year brand warranty. Accessories such as cables and cases usually carry a 30-day warranty. You can purchase extended warranty coverage during checkout.

Currently, we ship to the United States, Canada, the United Kingdom, and Australia. Shipping rates and delivery times vary depending on the destination country.

Standard domestic shipping takes between 3 to 5 business days. Express shipping takes 1 to 2 business days. International deliveries usually take 7 to 14 business days.

Orders & Payments

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and Google Pay. We also offer interest-free EMI options.

You can cancel or modify your order within 2 hours of placing it. After that, the order cannot be cancelled or modified.

Once your order has shipped, we will send your tracking number and carrier tracking link through email.

Returns & Refunds

We offer a 30-day return policy for unused items in original packaging. Defective products will be replaced free of charge.

Refunds are processed within 3 to 5 business days after inspection. Bank processing may take additional time.
